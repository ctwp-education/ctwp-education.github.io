Our Computer Lab in Zimbabwe

A fully equipped digital learning lab for disadvantaged and disabled children — powered by solar energy, Starlink internet, and the belief that every child deserves access to technology.

Joint Initiative

Stronger together — from idea to first lesson.CTWP Educational Empowerment × Bongai Shamwari e.V.

This computer lab is the result of two organisations sharing one conviction: that every child, regardless of where they are born, deserves access to quality digital education. CTWP Educational Empowerment and Bongai Shamwari e.V. pooled their expertise, networks, and resources to make it happen — from fundraising and procurement to the very first lesson.

Our Accomplishments

What We Built Together

From preparing the room to running the first lessons — here is what we achieved step by step.

Room Preparation

We furnished the lab with locally-made wooden desks and chairs, creating a proper classroom from scratch.

Done

Solar Energy

Solar panels were installed on the roof — the lab now runs entirely on clean, independent energy.

Done

Security

Burglar bars and CCTV cameras were fitted to protect all equipment around the clock.

Done

Starlink Internet

A Starlink satellite dish now gives the school fast, reliable internet — for the very first time.

Done

Computers Delivered

Computers were shipped from Europe and monitors sourced locally, fully equipping the lab.

Done

Technical Setup

Windows, educational software and child-safe browser settings were configured on every machine.

Done

ICT Teacher Hired

After interviews, a qualified ICT teacher was selected to run structured lessons full-time.

Done
