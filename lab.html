Thank You for Your Support!

"For disadvantaged and disabled children in Zimbabwe, Bongai Shamwari is far more than a place of learning — it is a source of hope, opportunity, and community."

Your generous contribution in 2025 has been instrumental in advancing the digitalization project at the Bongai Shamwari kindergarten and school. Thanks to your support, we have built sustainable digital infrastructure that opens the door to knowledge and new learning opportunities for the children, their teachers, and the wider community.

Your commitment is helping to shape a future in which every child can explore their potential and participate fully in the digital world. Each step brings us closer to our goal: making education accessible to all, regardless of external challenges.

Looking Ahead

Next Steps — 2026 & Beyond

The lab is running — now we need to sustain and grow it. Here's what comes next.

1

Secure Sponsorship for the ICT Teacher's Salary

Identify donors, partners, or CSR programs to fund the full-time ICT educator — ensuring long-term sustainability of the digital learning program.

2

Ship Remaining Equipment & Classroom Enhancements

Ship printer, projector, mouse pads, and USB sticks; purchase locally a shelf for storing equipment, dust-proof computer covers, and ICT schoolbooks.

3

Launch Full ICT Curriculum

Begin the first official teaching term of ICT as a structured subject at Bongai Shamwari — turning the pilot lessons into a complete digital education program.
