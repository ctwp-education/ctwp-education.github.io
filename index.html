About the Project

What We Do

Our Vision

Empower every child / individual on this planet through access to (free) education by using technology and thereby positively impact the world of us all.

Our Mission

Create an innovative school concept for underprivileged children that combines online and offline learning and integrates personalized education and real-life experiences.

Our Journey to Impact

Currently at Phase 4 out of 5

From discovering real needs across 60+ countries to building and improving a pilot school — watch our journey unfold or explore each phase below.

Completed
Phase 1

Discover

Understanding real needs before creating solutions

  • 150+ schools and universities visited across 60+ countries
  • Teachers and students trained in digital learning and AI tools
  • Global courses completed on pedagogy, learning science, and edtech
Completed
Phase 2

Design

Turning insights into a powerful learning model

  • Built a future-ready school concept (Prototype / MVP)
  • Combined online + offline learning experiences
  • Focused on personalized learning and real-life skills
Completed
Phase 3

Build

From vision to real-world impact

  • Pilot school launched
  • Safe and inspiring learning environment created
  • Model tested in everyday school life

Improve

  • Continuous testing and refinement
  • Feedback from students, teachers, and communities
  • Ongoing adaptation to increase impact
Upcoming

Scale

  • Strategic partnerships with schools and organizations
  • Model prepared for replication globally
  • Expanding access to meaningful education

Make an Impact

Support Us

Whether you're an individual or a company — there are many ways to contribute to our mission.

Donate

Every contribution directly funds technology access, teacher training, and learning materials for children in need.

Volunteer

Share your skills in tech, education, content creation, or marketing — remotely or on-site in Zimbabwe.

Become a Member

Join the Bongai Shamwari community. Your yearly membership forms the financial foundation we build on.

Spread the Word

Share our mission on social media and in your network. Awareness is the first step to meaningful change.

Corporate Sponsorship

Partner as a main sponsor like Trelleborg. Fund digital infrastructure, computer labs, and educational programs. Get visibility and impact reports.

Technology Donations

Donate laptops, tablets, or other devices to equip our computer labs and classrooms with the tools students need.

Employee Volunteering

Send skilled employees to train teachers and students in digital literacy, coding, and AI tools as part of your CSR program.

Strategic Partnership

Co-develop educational programs, provide software licenses, or support our scaling strategy across new regions.

Every Contribution Counts

100% of donations go directly to education programs. Bank transfers have zero transaction fees.
