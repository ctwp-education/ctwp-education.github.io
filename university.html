Higher Education Tools

Resources for University Students

Free platforms for research, open courseware, textbooks, productivity, and AI tools — everything you need to thrive in higher education.

Learn from the Best

Open Courseware & Lectures

MIT OpenCourseWare

Free lecture notes, problem sets, exams, and videos from MIT's full course catalog — no registration needed.

Visit ocw.mit.edu

Coursera (Audit Free)

Audit thousands of university-level courses from institutions like Yale, Duke, and Stanford at no cost.

Visit coursera.org

edX

Free courses from Harvard, MIT, and other top universities across engineering, business, humanities, and more.

Visit edx.org

MIT, Yale & Stanford on YouTube

Full university lecture series available free on YouTube — search by university and course name.

Visit youtube.com

NPTEL

High-quality engineering and science lectures from India's top IIT institutions, free and certifiable.

Visit nptel.ac.in
Read for Free

Free Textbooks & Academic Resources

OpenStax

Peer-reviewed, free textbooks for introductory college courses in science, math, economics, and more.

Visit openstax.org

Project Gutenberg

Over 70,000 free eBooks — essential for humanities, literature, and history students.

Visit gutenberg.org

Internet Archive — Books

Borrow millions of digitized books and academic texts online for free with a free account.

Visit archive.org

DOAJ — Open Access Journals

Directory of thousands of peer-reviewed open-access journals across all disciplines.

Visit doaj.org
Find & Cite

Research Tools

Google Scholar

Search millions of scholarly articles, theses, books, and conference papers across all fields.

Visit scholar.google.com

Semantic Scholar

AI-powered academic search engine that surfaces the most relevant and influential research papers.

Visit semanticscholar.org

Zotero

Free reference manager to collect, organize, cite, and share your research sources effortlessly.

Visit zotero.org

Connected Papers

Visualize the relationship between academic papers and discover relevant prior and derivative works.

Visit connectedpapers.com

RefSeek

Academic search engine covering over a billion documents from journals, encyclopedias, and universities.

Visit refseek.com
Study Smarter

AI Tools for University

ChatGPT

AI assistant for brainstorming, explaining complex topics, writing drafts, and exam preparation.

Visit chat.openai.com

Claude AI

Excellent for deep analysis, long-document understanding, essay feedback, and research summaries.

Visit claude.ai

NotebookLM

Upload your lecture notes and papers — AI summarizes, answers questions, and generates study guides.

Visit notebooklm.google.com

Perplexity AI

AI-powered search with cited sources — ideal for quick academic queries and literature reviews.

Visit perplexity.ai

Consensus

Search and summarize findings from peer-reviewed research papers using AI — great for literature reviews.

Visit consensus.app
Write & Organise

Productivity & Writing

Notion

All-in-one workspace for notes, project planning, databases, and collaboration — free for students.

Visit notion.so

Obsidian

Powerful linked note-taking app for building your personal knowledge base — free for personal use.

Visit obsidian.md

Overleaf

Free online LaTeX editor for writing academic papers, theses, and scientific reports collaboratively.

Visit overleaf.com

Grammarly

AI-powered writing assistant for grammar, clarity, and style checks in essays and assignments.

Visit grammarly.com

Anki

Spaced-repetition flashcard app used by medical and law students worldwide — free and highly effective.

Visit apps.ankiweb.net
Build & Analyse

Coding & Data Science

Google Colab

Free cloud-based Jupyter notebooks — run Python, machine learning, and data analysis with no setup.

Visit colab.research.google.com

Kaggle

Free datasets, notebooks, and competitions to learn data science and machine learning hands-on.

Visit kaggle.com

freeCodeCamp

Free full-stack web development curriculum with certifications in JavaScript, Python, and data analysis.

Visit freecodecamp.org

GitHub (Student Pack)

Version control and collaboration platform — students get free access to premium tools via GitHub Education.

Visit github.com
